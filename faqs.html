How do I change my subscription?

In order to change your subscription, you should first cancel your existing subscription. Click the person icon at the top right side of your screen and select 'Account Settings'. Click 'Cancel Subscription'. Once you've completed this task, Click 'Subscribe' in the the left menu and choose the new plan you want to purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime. When you cancel, you will no longer be able to chat so we suggest cancelling towards the end of your paid month/year to get the most out of Essential Chat. Click the person icon at the top right side of your screen and select 'Account Settings'. Click 'Cancel Subscription'.
