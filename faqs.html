Is Essential Chat safe to use for medical advice?

Essential Chat is designed for educational and informational purposes only. Always consult your healthcare provider for medical advice or treatment.

If I have more questions about protocols, products or anything in particular, who can I speak to?

We always recommend reaching out to the Wellness Advocate who enrolled you — they’re your best resource for personalized support and guidance. Not sure who that is? Contact doTERRA’s Customer Service team and they’ll help you find the right person.

Can Essential Chat recommend doTERRA products only?

Yes! Essential Chat focuses specifically on doTERRA products to help you get the best guidance tailored to what you already love and use.

Is there a limit to how many questions I can ask?

Nope! You can ask as many questions as you want — Essential Chat is here for you anytime.

Do I need special training to use Essential Chat?

Not at all! If you can type a question, you can use Essential Chat. It’s designed to be simple and intuitive for everyone.

Can I access Essential Chat on my phone or tablet?

Yes! Essential Chat is fully mobile-friendly, so you can ask questions anytime, anywhere.

How often is Essential Chat updated?

Essential Chat stays up-to-date to deliver the most current information about doTERRA products, safety guidelines, and wellness topics.

How do I change my subscription?

In order to change your subscription, you should first cancel your existing subscription. Click the person icon at the top right side of your screen and select 'Account Settings'. Click 'Cancel Subscription'. Once you've completed this task, Click 'Subscribe' in the the left menu and choose the new plan you want to purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime. When you cancel, you will no longer be able to chat so we suggest cancelling towards the end of your paid month/year to get the most out of Essential Chat. Click the person icon at the top right side of your screen and select 'Account Settings'. Click 'Cancel Subscription'.

Do you offer refunds?

We do offer refunds if you've purchased by mistake. Please contact us within 48 hours of your purchase and we'll provide a refund. We do not offer any other refunds after the 48 hour timeframe.
