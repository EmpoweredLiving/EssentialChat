What are Product Information Pages (PIPs)?

Product Information Pages, or PIPs, are detailed documents created by doTERRA for each of their products. Think of them as your comprehensive guide, offering in-depth insights that go beyond the label. Each PIP provides a wealth of knowledge, including key ingredients, primary benefits, directions for use, and any necessary cautions.

Why PIPs are an Amazing Resource (Especially for Supplements!):

Understanding what you're using is key to a confident wellness journey, and PIPs empower you with that knowledge!

  • Deep Product Knowledge: Discover the science, sourcing, and specific benefits of each essential oil, blend, or supplement.
  • Supplement Savvy: For supplements, PIPs are invaluable. They often detail the nutritional information, the purpose of key ingredients, and research supporting their efficacy, helping you make informed choices for your health goals.
  • Usage Guidance: Get clear instructions on how to use products safely and effectively for the best results.
  • Ingredient Transparency: Know exactly what's in your doTERRA products.
  • Quick Answers: A reliable first stop for many common product questions.

Whether you're new to an oil or looking to understand a supplement more deeply, PIPs are your go-to resource for official, detailed information.
